Who we are

Mount Wilson Capital has raised funds from individual investors to execute lending operations that help borrowers in unique and specific scenarios have the necessary funds in order to close their real estate transactions. Our funds primary goals are to take advantage of the premiums associated with lending to our customers and ability to mitigate risks through our customer Data Analysis.

We approach a lead or a customer reaches out to our team in order to secure a loan for a prospective property. After filling out the least amount of information needed, we can determine if this customer fits our investment requirements and interest. our concentration in building our client base will be to make this process as hassle free as possible. ultimately customers in this industry are looking for the most simplistic and efficientprocess and strongest customer service.
